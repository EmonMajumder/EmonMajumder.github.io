About Me

Dancer & Model

Alka Das Pranti is a renowned Indian Classical Dancer, choreographer and a model of the new age Bangladesh. She is trained in Odishi under the guidance of Guru Sharmila Biswas at Odissi Vision and Movement, India. Though, she studied Chemical Engineering from Jadavpur University under ICCR scholarship, she completely devoted herself to dance. Alka is one of the most determined youngsters of Bangladesh who took the courage to devote herself completely into dance and modelling, despite of having an engineering degree.
